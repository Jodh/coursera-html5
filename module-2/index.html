Lost in Philly

Vincent

A human bard who just came into his powers due to his innate white dragon bloodline. He is a measured and cautious individual and an expert strategist and team player. He makes sure that his party survive every encounter and no one is left behind. He loves the art of story telling and incorporates personal experiences into his performances.

Takara

A kind hearted but brave rogue that likes to stay out of trouble and hone her jewel crafting and appraising skills. She dual wields her shortsword and dagger in battle with deadly precision, only going in for kills when the right opportunity arrives. Her signature move is striking with two blades from the blind spot of her enemies.

Ziggraut

A curious and easy going loxodont Paladin that cares about the safety of his party. Ziggraut is down to try anything once. His love for his friends is only rivalled by his love for money and glory. He is an ex-gladiator out for gold and accolades. His signature move is punching his enemies with divinely empowered brass knuckles.
